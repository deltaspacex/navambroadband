Our Story

For nearly three decades, our core mission has remained unchanged: keeping our community connected to what matters most. Today, we don't just distribute internet; we build and manage the network infrastructure powering our city's future.

1998

Humble Beginnings

Founded in 1998, we began our journey as a dedicated local cable operator, bringing entertainment and quality service to the living rooms of Kollam. For nearly three decades, our core mission has remained unchanged: keeping our community connected to what matters most.

2015

The Digital Leap

As the digital landscape shifted, we stepped up to meet the demand for data. In 2015, we began delivering high-speed broadband connections across the region, building a reputation for fast, uninterrupted local support.

2025

Technological Independence

Driven by a commitment to true technological independence, we reached a major milestone in 2025 by officially launching as an independent Internet Service Provider (ISP)—making us the 1st local ISP originating right here in Kollam.

Present Day

Empowering Kollam's Future

Today, with 28 years of community trust and a growing family of 2,000+ satisfied customers, we don't just distribute internet; we build and manage the network infrastructure powering our city's future.
